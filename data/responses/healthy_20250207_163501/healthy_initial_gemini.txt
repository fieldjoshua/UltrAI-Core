**Supplements for Improved Circulation and Energy Levels**

**1. L-Arginine:**
* Improves blood vessel dilation, increasing circulation and oxygen delivery to muscles.

**2. Ginkgo Biloba:**
* Enhances blood flow to the brain and extremities, improving cognitive function and circulation.

**3. Vitamin C:**
* Supports collagen production, which strengthens blood vessels and improves blood flow.

**4. Magnesium:**
* Regulates blood pressure and improves circulation by relaxing blood vessels.

**5. Coenzyme Q10 (CoQ10):**
* Helps produce energy in cells, improving oxygen utilization and reducing fatigue.

**6. Iron:**
* Essential for transporting oxygen in red blood cells, improving circulation and energy levels.

**7. B Vitamins (B1, B2, B3, B6, B9, B12):**
* Support cellular energy production and improve blood flow.

**8. Omega-3 Fatty Acids:**
* Reduce inflammation and help maintain healthy blood vessels, improving circulation.

**9. Green Tea Extract:**
* Contains antioxidants that protect blood vessels and improve circulation.

**10. Grape Seed Extract:**
* Supports blood vessel health and reduces oxidation, improving circulation.

**Dosage and Safety:**

* Always consult with a healthcare professional before taking any supplements.
* Follow recommended dosages on product labels or as directed by your doctor.
* Avoid taking multiple supplements with the same ingredients or exceeding recommended amounts.
* Consider a comprehensive blood test to identify any underlying nutrient deficiencies before starting supplementation.
