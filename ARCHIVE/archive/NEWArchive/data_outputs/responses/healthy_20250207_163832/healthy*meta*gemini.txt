**Optimized Response:**

Maintaining optimal circulation and energy levels requires a balanced diet, regular exercise, and appropriate supplementation. Before incorporating supplements into your regimen, it's crucial to consult with a healthcare professional for personalized guidance based on your health history.

**Over-the-Counter Supplements for Circulation and Energy:**

1. **Omega-3 Fatty Acids:** Found in fish oil, omega-3s enhance heart health and circulation by reducing triglyceride levels, lowering blood pressure, and minimizing heart disease risk.
2. **Coenzyme Q10 (CoQ10):** An antioxidant that promotes cellular growth, CoQ10 supports energy production, improves exercise performance, and enhances circulation.
3. **Magnesium:** Essential for muscle and nerve function, blood sugar regulation, and blood pressure control, magnesium supplements aid sleep quality, which can positively impact energy levels.
4. **Vitamin B12:** Crucial for red blood cell formation, energy production, and nerve function, vitamin B12 supplements prevent anemia, a condition that causes fatigue and weakness.
5. **Iron:** Iron is necessary for oxygen transport, and supplements can boost energy levels by enhancing the blood's ability to carry oxygen. Consult with a healthcare professional before taking iron supplements, as excessive intake can be harmful.
6. **L-Arginine:** This amino acid converts to nitric oxide, which widens blood vessels for improved blood flow. While it may support circulation, evidence is mixed, and potential side effects exist. Seek professional advice before use.
7. **Ginseng:** Traditionally used in medicine, ginseng may enhance energy levels. Studies on its efficacy for energy and exercise performance yield generally positive results.
8. **Ashwagandha:** An adaptogen that helps reduce stress, improve energy levels, and support overall health, ashwagandha is thought to have numerous benefits, including anxiety reduction and improved brain function.

**Dosage and Cautions:**

* Follow manufacturer's instructions or consult with a healthcare professional for specific dosage recommendations.
* Start with a low dose and gradually increase as needed.
* Consider combining supplements for synergistic effects.
* Always consult with a healthcare professional before taking any supplements, especially if you have underlying health conditions or are taking medications.
* Supplements may interact with medications or cause side effects.
* Choose high-quality supplements from reputable brands.
